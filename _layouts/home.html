VFD Watch Face

A Wear OS Watch Face with intentionally pixelated time display part for retro VFD feeling. The ambient mode displays AM/PM indication instead of seconds. The default color scheme is amber-yellow-red-vermilion because some AMOLED's blue color tend to age quicker, thus we may extend the life of the display by going red hue colors (otherwise I'd have gone cyan style to mimick some VFDs better). With v2.0 green and blue color schemes can be selected as well. The layout offers 6 complication slots in two rows: one row is above the center time display and another row is below. All the complication slots are for small data. Source code

Flower Complication Watch Face

A Wear OS Watch Face is comprised of complications only. The complication slots are all equal size of 1/3rd of the whole watch face and aligned in a flower shape. There are 7 complication slots available this way. It's completely up to you what data you want to display, even including the time. I use an amber / vermilion / yellow / brown / red color scheme by default, avoiding blue which may age faster in some AMOLED displays. However blue and green schemes are available as well.
